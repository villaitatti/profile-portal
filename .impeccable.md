## Design Context

### Users
I Tatti staff, IT staff, and appointees/fellows use this portal to view profile data, access internal applications, and handle VIT ID administration. Most sessions are task-focused and repeat over time rather than exploratory.

### Brand Personality
Institutional, warm, human. The product should feel trustworthy and calm, not cold enterprise SaaS and not decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-mode product UI with warm neutrals, restrained crimson accents, stronger body readability, and quieter chrome. Main-content legibility takes priority over visual flourish. Avoid generic admin-dashboard tropes, cold gray surfaces, loud glows, and heavy nav treatments.

### Design Principles
- Let content outrank chrome: navigation should support the task, not dominate it.
- Favor readable body copy and clear hierarchy over tiny metadata.
- Warm the neutral palette and use accent color with restraint.
- Keep administrative workflows calm, clear, and humane.
- Prefer consistent shared patterns over one-off styling.
